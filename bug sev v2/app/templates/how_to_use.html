How to Use?

  • Step 1: Choose your input method on the homepage. You can either enter the data manually, upload a CSV file, or upload a JSONL file.
  • Step 2: If you choose manual input, fill out the form with the project details and code metrics. If you upload a file, ensure it contains the necessary data in the correct format.
  • Step 3: Click "Next" to review your input. Make sure all information is correct before proceeding.
  • Step 4: Click "Run" to submit your data for prediction. The system will process your input and provide the results.
  • Step 5: View the prediction results, including the severity and probabilities of each bug class. Analyze the results to identify and address potential issues in your project.
